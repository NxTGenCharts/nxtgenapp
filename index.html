What's Next
Each session now includes an Analytics tab (equity curve, rolling win rate, R-multiple distribution, streaks, Monte Carlo) and an AI Review tab — open any session and use the tabs at the top. The Screenshot Gallery and Strategy Comparison below pull from every trade you've logged. A strategy that clears the bar (5+ tests, 55%+ win rate, 1.5+ profit factor) is flagged Strong Setup — one click on Save to Playbook promotes it into a real Model. Every session also shows up on your Calendar — tap the on any day to drill into that day's sessions, trades, and Chart Replay.
